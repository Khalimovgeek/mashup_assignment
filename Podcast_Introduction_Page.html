The First Mic

Welcome to The First Mic, a podcast made for absolute beginners who are curious about starting their own podcast or simply love understanding how podcasts are created.In this series, we break down everything—from choosing your first topic and microphone to recording, editing, and publishing your episodes—using simple language and real examples.

No technical background required. Just curiosity, creativity, and a willingness to learn.

About the Podcast

The First Mic is designed to guide beginners step-by-step into the world of podcasting. Each episode focuses on one small, practical concept so you never feel overwhelmed. Whether you want to start a personal podcast, a tech talk, or a storytelling series, this podcast helps you take your first confident steps.
